About TheRecipeBook

Our product is geared towards first generation Americans/immigrants and children of immigrants in an effort to reconnect with our cultural roots. Personally speaking, this concept came from the idea that our group comprises first generation Americans with little knowledge about the traditional cooking methods of certain dishes from our cultures, with many of the recipes being held very secretively culturally, either simply being memorized or put in antiquated packages such as old books or notes. So we want to provide a resource for this knowledge to become more accessible. Target Age Group 18-24 maybe. Around 20-25% of pop is first and second generation americans
